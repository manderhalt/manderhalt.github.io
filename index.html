Skills

What I can do and what I like to do!

Data Science

Analyzing data is a passion! Harvesting raw data, and wrangling it. Finding causalities and effects, trying to give the data a meaning, explaining and understanding. In one word, making the data more valuable. Python is an incredible partner for this. Combined with a higher theoretical knowledge in Statistics, Algebra and Machine Learning, there is nowhere you can't go!

Database Management

I started to use Big Data oriented and/or schema-less databases like MongoDB and Hadoop a few years ago. I like the power of distributed algorithms that scale to Big Data, full text search features and geospatial queries. They are giving us the ability to manage large amount of heterogeneous data, while it was still a conundrum a few years ago. Everything is moving so fast! I cannot wait to see the future.

Data Visualization

It is nice to know how to analyze and handle data. But to bring data to life and to make it beautiful, what is better than our modern web browsers capabilities? I like using Meteor or Django to build the core of interactive web applications. For visualizations, I could spend days and days creating charts and infographics with d3. These kinds of libraries help us to make the data science an Art!
